# Code of Conduct

This is a small project, but the basics still apply: treat people decently.

## The short version

Be respectful and constructive. Assume good faith. Argue about the work, not the person. Harassment,
personal attacks, discrimination, and the other obvious stuff aren't welcome here — not in issues,
pull requests, commit messages, or anywhere else connected to the project.

## Not OK

A non-exhaustive list: insults or demeaning comments; harassment of any kind, public or private;
discriminatory language or "jokes"; publishing someone's private information without their
permission; or sustained disruption of a discussion after being asked to stop.

## If something goes wrong

If you see or experience behaviour that crosses the line, email me at **jaswanth.surya007@gmail.com**.
I'll look into it and do what's reasonable — anywhere from a quiet word to removing comments or
blocking someone, depending on what happened. Reports stay confidential.

This is written in the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/),
trimmed down to something I'll actually enforce.
